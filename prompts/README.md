## 🛠️ How to Use This Library

Every category follows a standardized structure:
1.  **`index.md`**: The master template. Contains the "Persona-Driven" prompt, a list of variables to fill out, and pro-tips for refinement.
2.  **`eg.md`**: Realistic examples showing how to fill out the template for specific use cases.

### The "Persona-Driven" Methodology
Our prompts don't just "Ask" for results; they **Adopt** a specialized persona. Instead of asking for a logo, we act as a "Senior Brand Identity Designer." This ensures the AI utilizes domain-specific vocabulary and professional logic.
